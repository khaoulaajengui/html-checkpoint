about us

Technology is the result of accumulated knowledge and application of skills,
methods, and processes used in industrial production and scientific research.
Technology is embedded in the operation of all machines and electronic devices,
with or without detailed knowledge of their function, for the intended purpose of an organization.
The technologies of society consist of what is known as systems. Systems operate by obtaining an input, altering this input
through what is known as a process, and then producing an outcome that achieves the intended purpose of the system.
